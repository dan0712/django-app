Team

Industry Veterans

Steve Lockshin

Steve is a serial entrepreneur within the wealth advisory industry. In 1994 Lockshin founded Convergent Wealth Advisors (formerly CMS Financial Services) and served as CEO for 18 years, and resigned his daily duties to become Chairman in 2012. Under his leadership the company became one of the largest wealth advisory firms, perennially recognized as a top RIA to the ultra-affluent. Steve has been ranked #1 by Barron’s in their state (CA) and national rankings in numerous periods.

Steve was also an influencer in the technology side of the industry. In 1995, while developing what became Convergent, Steve founded CMS Reporting; now known as Fortigent, LLC, a leading provider of outsourced wealth management and reporting solutions. Fortigent was acquired by LPL Financial Holdings, Inc. in 2012.

Since turning over the reins as CEO of Convergent, Lockshin has focused his efforts on serving as a champion for the fiduciary standard. In September 2013, his book Get Wise to Your Advisor was published and offers investors insight to find a capable advisor who will put the client’s financial well-being first. Lockshin holds the Investment Management Consultants Association’s Certified Investment Management Analyst designation and the Investment Strategist Certificate.

Steve still serves many of his clients through his newest RIA venture, AdvicePeriod, while also playing an active role at Betasmartz Institutional where he is focused on enabling advisors to more efficiently operate their businesses and better serve their clients.

Marty Bicknell

Martin C. (Marty) Bicknell is the CEO of Mariner Holdings, LLC, the parent company of Mariner Wealth Advisors and Montage Investments.

Marty founded Mariner in 2006 with a team of just seven other individuals and $300 million in assets under management. Since then, he has led the firm as it has grown to over $35 billion in assets and 440 associates and has gained recognition for his industry leadership by being nationally ranked #2 on Barron’s list of the Top 100 Independent Wealth Advisors for 2013 and 2014.

Marty’s vision of fostering an industry-leading, national wealth advisory firm focused on helping clients achieve their goals has led to multiple acquisitions of advisory firms that share his vision, as well as the inception of multiple firms specifically focused on solving client challenges. His leadership has resulted in a firm comprised of industry-leading professionals providing a wide range of services, including, but not limited to: wealth advice and financial planning, asset management, trust and estate planning, retirement planning, investment banking and advisory services, tax consulting, insurance consulting, and more.

As a recognized leader in the field of financial problem-solving for companies and their executives, Marty has been a valuable resource for other successful entrepreneurs, including supporting efforts with the development of Betasmartz Institutional.

Prior to founding the firm in 2006, Marty was a senior vice president of investments at A.G. Edwards & Sons, where he led a staff of professional financial consultants in providing customized wealth management solutions for public and private corporations, high-net worth individuals and their families, and charitable organizations.

Digital Leaders

Jon Stein

Jon is the founder and CEO of Betasmartz, managing the strategy and overall direction for the company.

Previously, Jon spent his career developing financial products, platforms, and investment strategies for a number of the world’s most prominent financial institutions. As a Consultant at First Manhattan Consulting Group for half a decade, Jon developed in-depth knowledge of retail banking, investing, and risk management.

Seeing an investment management landscape cluttered with inefficient products that either did little to help investors or actively hurt their performance, Jon set out to create a sophisticated and accessible investment service that would apply technology thoughtfully to address customers’ investing goals. Passionate about efficiency, customer happiness, and helping people make smart decisions with their money, he founded Betasmartz in 2008.

He is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and a FINRA Series 7, 24, and 63 Registered Securities Representative. Jon is a graduate of Harvard University and Columbia Business School.

Eli Broverman

Eli is the co-founder and COO of Betasmartz, overseeing the implementation of all legal and regulatory requirements. He is in charge of account management and trading operations, and also works on the development of partnerships and strategic opportunities for the company.

Prior to co-founding Betasmartz, Eli was an attorney with Proskauer Rose LLP. Eli specialized in ERISA law, working closely with Fortune 500 companies and Wall Street banks.

His passion for applying the law to novel and innovative businesses helped Eli develop an expertise in the area of investment management and its implementation. Based on his experience, he quickly saw a path for how Betasmartz could be structured to best achieve investment objectives for customers and help make their lives better.

Eli graduated from Brown University and holds a J.D. from NYU School of Law.
