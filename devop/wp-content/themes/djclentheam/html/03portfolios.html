Portfolios

Betterment Institutional provides portfolio solutions for Advisors looking for a comprehensive and diversified approach.

We invest in up to 12 global asset classes, each represented by an ETF, ranging from short-term Treasuries to international bonds and emerging market equities.
Each ETF in the portfolio tracks a broad, well-established index, is liquid, and has a low expense ratio. This makes our portfolio one of the best values available to your clients.

 

Stock ETFs

  • Vanguard U.S. Total Stock Market Index (VTI)
  • Vanguard US Large-Cap Value Index (VTV)
  • Vanguard US Mid-Cap Value Index (VOE)
  • Vanguard US Small-Cap Value Index (VBR)
  • Vanguard FTSE Developed Market Index (VEA)
  • Vanguard FTSE Emerging Index (VWO)

Bond ETFs

Secondary and tertiary ETFs are also made available to clients using our Tax Loss Harvesting+ feature.
